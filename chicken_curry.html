Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  3. Stir in curry powder and turmeric, coating the chicken evenly.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
