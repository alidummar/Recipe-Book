Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown chicken on both sides.
  5. Place browned chicken in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and basil.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve over pasta or with a side salad.
