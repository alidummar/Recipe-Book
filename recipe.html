Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
