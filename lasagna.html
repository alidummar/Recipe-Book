Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 9 cooked lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a greased baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat the layers.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  6. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving.
