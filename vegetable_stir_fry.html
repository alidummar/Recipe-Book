Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional for thickening)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add broccoli, bell peppers, carrot, and snap peas. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch (if using). Pour over the vegetables and toss to coat.
  4. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
